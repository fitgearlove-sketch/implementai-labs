## Email Best Practices

**Deliverability:**
- Use plain text or minimal HTML
- Keep under 500 words per email
- Avoid spam trigger words (free, guarantee, act now)
- Always include physical address in footer

**Personalization:**
- Use first name 2-3 times per email
- Reference agency name throughout
- Include specific details from audit/call
- Vary email length and style

**Timing:**
- Send during business hours (9am-5pm their timezone)
- Avoid Mondays (inbox overload) and Fridays (weekend mode)
- Best days: Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-2pm

**Tracking:**
- Track opens, clicks, replies
- A/B test subject lines
- Monitor which emails drive calls
- Adjust sequence based on engagement
