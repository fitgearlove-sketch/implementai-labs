# Skills Assessment & Readiness Check - ImplementAI Labs

## Founder Skills Evaluation

### Category 1: Business & Strategy (CRITICAL)

**✅ Agency Operations Knowledge**
- Do you understand how digital agencies operate?
- Have you worked in or closely with agencies before?
- Do you know common agency pain points firsthand?

**Assessment:** If NO to all three → HIGH RISK. Consider getting an agency operations co-founder or spending 3-6 months consulting for agencies first.

**✅ Business Model Understanding**
- Can you explain value-based pricing vs hourly pricing?
- Do you understand B2B sales cycles?
- Can you articulate ROI for business services?

**Assessment:** If NO to 2+ → MEDIUM RISK. Study B2B consulting business models before launching.

**✅ Strategic Thinking**
- Can you identify operational bottlenecks in a business?
- Can you prioritize solutions by impact vs effort?
- Can you create implementation roadmaps?

**Assessment:** If NO to 2+ → MEDIUM RISK. Practice by auditing 5-10 agencies (free) before selling.

### Category 2: Technical Capabilities (CRITICAL)

**✅ Workflow Automation (ESSENTIAL)**
- Proficient with Make.com, Zapier, or n8n?
- Can you build multi-step workflows with conditional logic?
- Understand webhooks, API calls, and data transformations?

**Assessment:** If NO to all three → HIGH RISK. Must learn before launching. Estimated learning time: 40-60 hours with tutorials.

**Resources:**
- Make.com Academy (free courses)
- "Automate Your Work" by Aytekin Tank
- YouTube: "Make.com tutorials for beginners"

**✅ AI & Prompt Engineering (ESSENTIAL)**
- Experience with Claude, GPT-4, or similar LLMs?
- Can you write effective prompts with clear instructions?
- Understand few-shot learning and output formatting?

**Assessment:** If NO to all three → MEDIUM-HIGH RISK. Must develop before delivering quality reports. Estimated learning time: 20-30 hours of experimentation.

**Resources:**
- Anthropic's Prompt Engineering Guide
- OpenAI Prompt Engineering documentation
- Experiment with 50+ prompts on real scenarios

**✅ Integration & APIs (IMPORTANT)**
- Comfortable reading API documentation?
- Can you authenticate and make API calls?
- Understand JSON data structures?

**Assessment:** If NO to all three → MEDIUM RISK. Can start with no-code tools but will limit growth. Learning time: 30-40 hours.

**✅ Basic Web Tech (HELPFUL)**
- Familiar with HTML/CSS for customization?
- Can you use Framer, Webflow, or similar no-code builders?
- Understand basic web analytics?

**Assessment:** If NO to all three → LOW RISK. Can use templates and hire for design. But basic understanding helps.

### Category 3: Marketing & Sales (CRITICAL)

**✅ Content Marketing (ESSENTIAL)**
- Can you write clearly and persuasively?
- Comfortable creating content (LinkedIn, YouTube, blog)?
- Understand SEO basics and content distribution?

**Assessment:** If NO to all three → HIGH RISK. This business requires authority building through content. Must develop or hire.

**Time Investment:** 10-15 hours/week for content creation and distribution.

**✅ Consultative Selling (ESSENTIAL)**
- Can you conduct discovery calls effectively?
- Comfortable asking about budgets and timelines?
- Can you handle objections without being pushy?

**Assessment:** If NO to all three → HIGH RISK. Must practice or partner with someone who can sell.

**Resources:**
- "The Challenger Sale" by Matthew Dixon
- "Selling to Big Companies" by Jill Konrath
- Practice with 10+ discovery calls before asking for money

**✅ Personal Brand Building (IMPORTANT)**
- Active on LinkedIn or similar professional platform?
- Willing to put yourself out there publicly?
- Can you network and build relationships?

**Assessment:** If NO to all three → MEDIUM RISK. This model relies on founder-led authority. Must overcome or pivot to different model.

### Category 4: Project Management & Delivery (IMPORTANT)

**✅ Client Management**
- Experience managing client expectations?
- Can you scope projects and set boundaries?
- Comfortable with contracts and agreements?

**Assessment:** If NO to all three → MEDIUM RISK. Will struggle with delivery and client satisfaction.

**✅ Documentation & Process**
- Do you naturally document your work?
- Can you create SOPs and training materials?
- Comfortable with project management tools?

**Assessment:** If NO to 2+ → LOW-MEDIUM RISK. Will limit scaling but not critical for early stage.

## Minimum Viable Skill Set (To Launch)

### Must-Have Skills:
1. **Workflow automation** (Make.com or Zapier) - 6/10 proficiency
2. **AI/LLM usage** (Claude or GPT) - 5/10 proficiency
3. **Content creation** (writing/video) - 6/10 proficiency
4. **Consultative selling** - 5/10 proficiency
5. **Agency operations knowledge** - 7/10 understanding

### Can Learn Along the Way:
- Advanced integrations
- Video editing
- SEO optimization
- Advanced prompt engineering
- CRM mastery

### Can Outsource/Partner:
- Web design (use Framer templates)
- Legal (hire lawyer for contracts)
- Accounting (use software + accountant)
- Graphic design (use Canva)

- [ ] Set up personal workflow automations (5 hours)

### Week 3-4: AI & Prompt Engineering (15 hours)
- [ ] Read Anthropic prompt engineering guide (3 hours)
- [ ] Experiment with 25+ prompts on audit scenarios (8 hours)
- [ ] Create 3 versions of "Report Brain" prompt (4 hours)

### Week 5-6: Content & Marketing (15 hours)
- [ ] Study 10 successful agency consultants' content (5 hours)
- [ ] Write 10 practice LinkedIn posts (5 hours)
- [ ] Script and record 2 practice YouTube videos (5 hours)

### Week 7-8: Sales & Client Management (10 hours)
- [ ] Read 1 sales book cover-to-cover (6 hours)
- [ ] Create discovery call script and practice (4 hours)

**Total Pre-Launch Learning:** 60 hours (7-8 weeks at 8 hours/week)

## Critical Skills Gaps - Mitigation Strategies

### Gap: Limited Technical Skills
**Solutions:**
1. Partner with technical co-founder (equity split)
2. Hire freelance automation specialist (per-project)
3. Intensive bootcamp (60-80 hours over 4 weeks)

### Gap: No Sales Experience
**Solutions:**
1. Partner with someone who can sell (revenue split)
2. Sales coaching (1-3 months)
3. Start with warm outreach to reduce sales pressure

### Gap: No Agency Experience
**Solutions:**
1. Work with 5-10 agencies (free audits) to learn
2. Partner with ex-agency owner
3. Deep research + client interviews (validate assumptions)

### Gap: Fear of Content Creation
**Solutions:**
1. Start with written content only (LinkedIn)
2. Hire content strategist/ghostwriter
3. Focus on case studies vs thought leadership

## Go/No-Go Decision Framework

### GREEN LIGHT (Launch Now):
- 7+ on workflow automation
- 6+ on AI/LLM usage
- 6+ on content creation
- 5+ on consultative selling
- 7+ on agency knowledge
- 60+ hours available per month

### YELLOW LIGHT (Prepare First):
- 5-6 on critical technical skills
- 4-5 on marketing/sales
- Some agency knowledge but not deep
- 40-60 hours available per month
- **Action:** 4-8 weeks skill building

### RED LIGHT (Reconsider or Partner):
- <5 on multiple critical skills
- No agency experience
- <40 hours available per month
- Not willing to learn technical skills
- **Action:** Find co-founder or pivot
