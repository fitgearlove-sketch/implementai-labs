# Content Marketing Strategy - ImplementAI Labs

## Core Content Philosophy

**Authority > Volume**
Build deep expertise in one niche rather than shallow coverage of everything AI. Each piece should demonstrate you've solved real agency problems, not just regurgitated theory.

**Show, Don't Tell**
Share actual automations, real cost savings, specific workflows. Avoid generic "AI can help your business" fluff.

**Async-First Distribution**
Focus on platforms where content has long-term discoverability (YouTube, SEO blog posts) rather than ephemeral content (Stories, TikTok).

## LinkedIn Strategy

### Profile Optimization
- **Headline:** "Helping Digital Agencies Reclaim 20% Profit Lost to Manual Work | AI Automation Specialist"
- **About:** Story-driven (founder background → agency pain witnessed → solution built)
- **Featured:** Free audit link, best case studies, popular content
- **Banner:** Clear value prop + CTA

### Content Calendar (2-3 posts/week)

**Monday:** Industry insight or trend analysis
**Wednesday:** How-to or tutorial (screenshot/video)
**Friday:** Case study or client win

**Post Types:**
1. **Carousels** (10 slides, tactical how-to)
2. **Text-only deep dives** (1,200+ characters, storytelling)
3. **Video walkthroughs** (Loom recording of automation)
4. **Polls** (engagement, audience research)

**Engagement Strategy:**
- Comment on 5-10 target prospect posts daily
- Reply to all comments within 2 hours
- DM thoughtful responses to engaged followers
- Share others' content with commentary

## Metrics to Track

### Vanity Metrics (Don't Obsess)
- Followers, likes, views

### Business Metrics (Track Weekly)
- Audit form completions
- Discovery call bookings
- Content-to-conversion rate
- Traffic source breakdown

### Content Performance Indicators
- LinkedIn: Comments + shares (not just likes)
- YouTube: Watch time % + CTR
- Blog: Time on page + conversion rate
- Email: Open rate + click rate

## Content Differentiation

### What Makes You Different
- **Specific Numbers:** "$47K wasted" vs "lots of waste"
- **Niche Focus:** Agencies only, not "all businesses"
- **Tactical Depth:** Full tutorials vs surface-level tips
- **Real Examples:** Named case studies vs hypotheticals
- **No Hype:** Honest about limitations and effort required

### Voice & Tone Guidelines
- **Professional but conversational** - Not corporate, not too casual
- **Data-driven but human** - Numbers + stories
- **Confident but not arrogant** - "Here's what works" not "I'm the best"
- **Practical over theoretical** - Always answer "how" not just "why"
