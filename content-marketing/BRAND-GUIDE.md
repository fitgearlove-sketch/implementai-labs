# ImplementAI Labs — Brand Guide

## Brand Identity

**Mission:** Help performance marketing agencies recover the 20% of profit they're losing to manual work.

**Tagline:** *Recover the 20% of Profit You're Losing to Manual Ad Ops*

**Niche:** Performance Marketing agencies serving E-commerce/DTC brands (5-30 employees)

## Visual Identity

### Colors

| Role | Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|------|-----|-------|
| Primary | Deep Space Black | `#0A0A0F` | Backgrounds, dark UI |
| Accent | Electric Blue | `#3B82F6` | CTAs, links, highlights |
| Text | White | `#FFFFFF` | Body text on dark bg |
| Muted | Light Gray | `#9CA3AF` | Secondary text, captions |

### Logo

- Black/white monochromatic icon (neural network node + circuit line)
- No text in icon, just the mark
- Logo on dark or light backgrounds depending on context

### Typography

- **Primary font:** Inter (weights: 400, 600, 700, 800)
- **Headings:** Bold/Extra Bold
- **Body:** Regular weight
- **Monospace:** For code, automation references

### Imagery Style

- Dark theme with high contrast
- Network, circuit, data-visualization motifs
- Flat, geometric, minimal — no gradients or gloss
- Unsplash style: tech abstracts, dark mode friendly

## Tone of Voice

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Confident | Not arrogant. You've done this before. Speak from experience. |
| Analytical | Data-driven. Every claim backed by a number. No vague promises. |
| ROI-focused | Everything framed in hours saved and dollars recovered. |
| Niche-specific | Always address performance marketing + e-commerce agencies. Never generic. |
| Pragmatic | "We implement, we don't just advise." Avoid overhyped AI promises. |

### Language Patterns

- **Do:** "Your team spends 12 hours on reporting. We can cut that to 90 minutes."
- **Don't:** "AI will revolutionize your business."
- **Do:** Use specific tool names (Meta Ads, Klaviyo, Shopify, GA4, Google Ads, TikTok Ads)
- **Don't:** Say "automation tools" without specifics
- **Numbers over adjectives:** "$47k/year" not "significant savings"

## Content Pillars (LinkedIn)

1. **Agency Ops Insights** — Data about agency inefficiencies, reporting time, lead response
2. **Automation How-To** — Specific workflows, tool integrations, before/after
3. **Profit Leak Analysis** — The real cost of manual processes in dollars and hours
4. **Founder's Perspective** — Building ImplementAI Labs, lessons learned, client results

## Content Formats

| Format | Best For | Frequency |
|--------|----------|-----------|
| Single post (text) | Quick insights, opinions, data points | 2x/week |
| Carousel (images) | Educational step-by-step, frameworks | 1x/week |
| PDF/document | Deep dives, templates, checklists | 1x/month |
| Video (recorded) | Personal connection, case studies | 1x/month |

## Profile Photo Guidelines

- Professional headshot on clean background
- Well-lit, recent (within 6 months)
- Looking at camera, friendly but confident expression
- No sunglasses, hats, or busy backgrounds
